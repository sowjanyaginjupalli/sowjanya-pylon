Group Health and Environment Policy

We firmly believe that we can grow to be one of the most responsible and remarkable Construction service providers to Industrial and Real Estate Sectors through continuous evolution, positive approach, upgrading of Management skills and committed team effort to enhance quality, competence and reliability.

We shall operate in a manner so that no people, property and the environment is harmed by Identifying, controlling and reducing all associated risks to the lowest practical levels.

We shall achieve this by:


  • Relentless improvement of Health, Safety and Environment system for all associated stake holders
  • Complying with all applicable legislations and HSE requirements.
  • Providing sufficient training and resources for effective HSE implementation.
  • Improving customer satisfaction by creating a safe and healthy working environment.
