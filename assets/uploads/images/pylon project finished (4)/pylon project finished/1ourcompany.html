Pylon Projects Private Limited (PPPL) was Established on May 14, 2010. PPPL specializes in execution of Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Piping, Electrical & Instrumentation, Pre- Commissioning & Commissioning works for Industrial and Real Estate Projects including Integrated Townships, Institutional Buildings, Warehouse, Datacenters etc. It’s core competency lies in on-time delivery of Projects for its Clients.

Pylon Projects Pvt. Ltd.(PPPL) is a subsidiary of Swastik Infralogics Pvt. Ltd.(SIPL), Mumbai, which has achieved a consistent turnover growth in last 10 years and achieved a turnover of more than Rs. 900 Crore in FY 2021-22 and has strong financials and ownership of large numbers of Construction equipments. Swastik Infralogics Pvt. Ltd.(SIPL) is involved in execution of Projects, which are essential for Urban Human life like

  • Road Infrastructures – National & State Highway (Rigid & Flexible Pavement)
  • Public Buildings – Hospital, School, Community Halls, Temples etc.
  • Underground Infrastructure – Water Supply, Sewerage, Storm Water drainage, STP etc.
  • Structures – Major & Minor Bridges, VUPs & LVUPs, Culverts, RE & Retaining Walls
  • Other Works – Immersion & holding ponds bus depots, parking lots, playgrounds, gardens, Reclamation Works and desilting of water bodies etc.

Swastik Infralogics Pvt. Ltd. has associated with Ajay Hans, former Managing Director of Petron Engineering Group to enhance/expand into the Industrial & Real Estate Core Operational sectors under a structure through it’s Subsidiary Pylon Projects Pvt. Ltd., Navi-Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. SWASTIK shall extend support for Technical, Financial & Commercial Qualifications, Banking, Finance, Equipment and other necessary support at Head office and site. Under Pylon Projects Pvt. Ltd., a team consisting of technical experts and specialist Civil, Mechanical and E&I is engaged majorly from former companies like Petron Civil & Petron Engineering and other similar companies to execute Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation works services to all Core Industrial and Real Estate operational sectors. The growth plans of Pylon Projects Pvt. Ltd. and Swastik Infralogics Pvt. Ltd. are complimentary and powerful synergy that exists to expand the activities of the group in PAN India markets across all our Industrial, Real Estate and Infrastructure sectors.
